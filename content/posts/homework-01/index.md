| Turkish Phrase   | Pronunciation        | English Translation | Context                         |
| :--------------- | :------------------- | :------------------ | :------------------------------ |
| **Merhaba**      | *Mer-ha-ba*          | Hello               | Standard greeting at any time.  |
| **Günaydın**     | *Goon-eye-din*       | Good morning        | Used only in the morning.       |
| **İyi günler**   | *Ee-yee goon-ler*    | Good day            | Greeting or farewell (daytime). |
| **Hoş geldiniz** | *Hosh gel-din-iz*    | Welcome             | Said to someone arriving.       |
| **İyi akşamlar** | *Ee-yee ak-sham-lar* | Good evening        | Greeting or farewell (evening). |
| **Hoşça kal**    | *Hosh-cha kal*       | Goodbye             | Said by the person **leaving**. |
