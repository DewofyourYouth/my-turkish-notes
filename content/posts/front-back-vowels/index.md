Turkish has two groups of vowels. Back vowels and front vowels

## Back Vowels (Pluralized with LAR)

**Aa Iı Oo Uu**

## Front Vowels (Pluralized with LER)

**Ee İi Öö Üü**

It goes based on the **LAST** vowel in the word.
